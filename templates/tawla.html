How to play Tawla 31
  • All 15 of your checkers start stacked on your head point. You run them the long way round into your home board, then bear them off. Your opponent runs the opposite way.
  • No hitting: a point holding even one enemy checker is blocked.
  • The runner: your first checker must reach your home board before any other checker may move.
  • You may not lock the opponent in completely.
  • 2-minute turns: if your time runs out, the computer finishes the round for you.
  • Bear off all 15 first: you score 1 point per checker the opponent still has on the board. First to 31 points wins the match.
