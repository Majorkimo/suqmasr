How to play Tawla 31
  • All 15 of your cream checkers start stacked on point 24 (top right). You move them the long way round to points 6→1, then bear them off. The computer runs the opposite way.
  • No hitting: a point holding even one enemy checker is blocked — you can never land there.
  • The runner: your first checker must reach your home board (points 1–6) before any other checker may move.
  • You may not lock the opponent in completely — a move that leaves them no possible move with any dice is not allowed.
  • Bear off all 15 first to win the round: you score 1 point per checker the opponent still has on the board (up to 15). First to 31 points wins the match.
